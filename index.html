Why are fruites and vegetables important?

Fruits and vegetables are an essential part of a healthy diet. They are packed with vitamins, minerals,antioxidants, and other nutrients that our bodies need to function properly.

Fruits and vegetables can help to reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, stroke, cancer, and diabetes. They can also help to boost the immune system, improve digestion, and maintain a healthy weight.

How much fruit and vegetables should i eat?

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) recommends that adults eat at least 1.5 to 2 cups of fruit and 2 to 3 cups of vegetables per day.

Here are some tips for getting more fruits and vegetables into your diet:

  • Add fruits and vegetables to every meal and snack
  • Choose a variety of fruits and vegetables in different colors
  • Make fruits and vegetables convenient by washing and sutting them ahead of time
  • Keep fruits and vegetables visible in your home so that you are more likely to eat them
